About Anna

Hi! I am a web developer based in Vancouver, BC. I find beauty in creating tools that work seamlessly. That said, all I want is to do meaningful work and help make your life easier. I also believe a tool should look as good as it works.

Although my background is in business (Management of Information Systems), I have always had a passion for programming and design. My past 6 years of work in the freight and transportation industry has helped me mature into the well rounded web developer I am today.

I believe in hard work, neat visuals, meaningful work, and personal growth.
