About Anna

Hi, I'm Anna. I am a web developer and database programmer. I find beauty in creating tools that work seamlessly. That said, all I want is to do meaningful work and help make your life easier. I also believe a tool should look as good as it works. This could be your website, database, shell script, or spreadsheet.

My educational background in business process management and information systems management with a side study interactive arts and design. My 5 years of working in an operations role in the freight and transportation industry make me a well rounded and goal-driven developer and programmer.

I believe in hard work, neat visuals, meaningful work, and personal growth.
