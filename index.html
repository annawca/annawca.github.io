About The branding, logo, and work-in-progress elements for the website for the clinic. “Style” is run by a mother-daughter duo that specializes in women’s health, acute health, and weight-loss. They approached me for ways to connect with prospective clients and to share their expertise on a blog. Discovery I met with “Style” to consult a marketing plan and discussed how they can share their expertise over social media and on their upcoming blog. From the design process, I learned that reviewing powerpoint slides together was most effective in realizing “Style” ‘s goals and preferences. The slides were of visual element designs that I chose for them and also examples used by their competitors in the clinical industry. From here on “Style” prefers a graphic logo and I will be using Inkscape to create their original logo design. Ruby on Rails will be used to build their bilingual English/Chinese website and blog.
